Double Check ("the Software") is a browser extension that assists users in
verifying values they enter into web forms. It is an assistive tool: it
helps you perform verification; it does not perform verification for you.

Double Check does not and cannot guarantee that any value is correct. Format
and checksum validation can confirm that a value is internally consistent,
not that it is the right value. A verification "match" means your two
readings of your source agreed — it does not mean the source itself was
correct, current, or authentic.

**You remain solely responsible for every value you submit**, including
